Social Media Growth Audit

Agency: biic.com.bd
Website: https://biic.com.bd
Date: 26 February 2026

Website: 7/10
Social: 4/10
Overall: 5/10

Key Growth Gaps

  • 1. **Website Branding**: The branding elements are incomplete, and there is no clear visual identity or message.
  • 2. **Social Media Presence**: There are no social media accounts to engage with potential customers and build a community.
  • 3. **Message and Value Proposition**: The website lacks a clear message or purpose that would attract visitors.
  • 4. **Call-to-Action (CTA)**: The website does not have a compelling CTA to encourage action from visitors.

Quick Wins

  • 1. **Create a Clear Branding Strategy**: Develop a clear brand identity with a unique visual identity that reflects your agency’s value proposition.
  • 2. **Build Social Media Accounts**: Create social media accounts on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok to engage with potential customers and build a community.
  • 3. **Develop a Message and Value Proposition**: Clearly communicate the benefits of using the agency’s services to potential customers. This will help them understand why they should choose you over competitors.
  • 4. **Create a Compelling CTA**: Design a clear and compelling CTA that encourages action from visitors, such as signing up for newsletters or contacting sales.
