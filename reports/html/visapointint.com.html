Social Media Growth Audit

Agency: visapointint.com
Website: https://visapointint.com
Date: 26 February 2026

Website: 5/10
Social: 5/10
Overall: 5/10

Key Growth Gaps

  • 1. **Improved Visual Presence**: The website could benefit from more visually appealing graphics and images to make it stand out from competitors.
  • 2. **Engaging Content**: The content on social media is mostly informative, which can be improved by incorporating more interactive elements like quizzes or polls to keep users engaged.
  • 3. **Consistency in Information**: Although the company provides accurate information about visa applications, consistency in updates and changes could enhance user confidence.
  • 4. **Local SEO**: The company could improve its local search engine optimization (SEO) to better rank higher on organic searches related to immigration services in Bangladesh.

Quick Wins

  • 1. **Add Visual Elements**: Incorporate high-quality images of successful visa applications, student life abroad, and other engaging content to make the site more visually appealing.
  • 2. **Engage with Users**: Host quizzes or polls related to immigration services to keep users engaged and interested in learning more about the company's offerings.
  • 3. **Update Content Regularly**: Keep the content on social media fresh and relevant by regularly updating information about visa applications, student life abroad, and other important topics.
  • 4. **Optimize for Local SEO**: Conduct local research and optimization (SEO) to better rank higher on organic searches related to immigration services in Bangladesh.
