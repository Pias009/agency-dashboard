Social Media Growth Audit

Agency: www.hsia.gov.bd
Website: https://www.hsia.gov.bd
Date: 26 February 2026

Website: 5/10
Social: 5/10
Overall: 5/10

Key Growth Gaps

  • 1. **Limited Social Media Presence:** The website does not have any social media links or profiles listed.
  • 2. **Inclusive Content:** While the website covers a broad range of topics, there is no mention of inclusive content for guests with disabilities.
  • 3. **Customer Reviews and Feedback:** There are no customer reviews or feedback sections mentioned on the site.
  • 4. **SEO Optimization:** The website lacks proper SEO optimization, which can affect its visibility in search engine results.

Quick Wins

  • 1. **Social Media Integration:** Add social media links to the footer of the website and make sure they are clearly visible.
  • 2. **Inclusive Content:** Include resources for guests with disabilities on the website.
  • 3. **Customer Reviews and Feedback:** Establish a customer review section where customers can provide their feedback and reviews.
  • 4. **SEO Optimization:** Optimize the website's content and meta tags to improve visibility in search engine results.
