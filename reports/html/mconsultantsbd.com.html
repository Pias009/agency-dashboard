Social Media Growth Audit

Agency: mconsultantsbd.com
Website: https://mconsultantsbd.com
Date: 26 February 2026

Website: 9/10
Social: 8/10
Overall: 7/10

Key Growth Gaps

  • 1. **Clear Call-to-Action (CTA)**: A prominent call-to-action button or link should be clearly placed on the homepage and throughout the site, directing users directly to the consultation process.
  • 2. **Engaging Content**: While the website has a good overview of services, there could be more engaging content such as testimonials, case studies, or blog posts to showcase the agency's success stories.
  • 3. **Accessibility**: The website lacks some accessibility features such as alt text for images and proper color contrast for different elements. This can affect users with disabilities.
  • 4. **User-Friendly Contact Form**: A user-friendly contact form should be accessible via email, phone number, or social media platforms to ensure easy communication with potential clients.

Quick Wins

  • The list of programs is extensive but could be more organized and easy to find for users.
